About AI You Can Use

Helping real people use AI tools without the hype, fear, or jargon.

Why this exists

AI tools like ChatGPT are everywhere now, but most people still aren’t sure how to use them in a way that actually helps with real life. There’s a lot of hype, a lot of buzzwords, and not a lot of clear, simple guidance.

AI You Can Use is my way of fixing that: short videos, clear articles, and practical resources that show you exactly how to use AI at work and at home.

Who I am

I’m Joe — a talent acquisition pro, creator, and long-time gamer who loves turning complex tools into practical workflows that normal people can actually use.

I spend my days helping people find great jobs, and my nights experimenting with new tools, workflows, and ways to save time with AI. This site is the place where all of that learning gets turned into step-by-step guides for you.

What you’ll find here

  • Videos that show you how to use AI tools in real scenarios.
  • Blog posts that break down concepts in simple language.
  • Free resources like prompt packs, templates, and tool recommendations I’ve personally tested.

Who this is for

If you’ve ever thought, “I know I should be using AI more, but I don’t know where to start,” this is for you.

You don’t need to be “technical.” You just need to be curious and open to trying new workflows that can make your day a little easier.
